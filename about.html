About Me

I originally trained in Movement Psychology and Character analysis; developed by Yat Malmgren and Christopher Fettes, Founders of Drama centre London and I trained directly under Christopher. I got my first agent out of my showcase and have now been a working / professional actor for almost 10 years. After graduating I enjoyed some reasonable successes but I found myself increasingly always pencilled but never booked. Something wasn’t working so I had to try and fix it. I’d always wanted to train with Ivana Chubbuck. Having always been a Brad Pitt fanboy - I had gotten my hands on Ivana's book many years prior. I then went full circle and started to pursue training with Ivana and working with the chubbuck technique. i solely and fully subscribe to the chubbuck technique. After my first few months working with the Chubbuck Technique, I booked five jobs back to back and spent almost half a year shooting all over Europe (Spain, Switzerland and Italy). Working with Ivana has been nothing but life-changing and as an actor, I still work with her today! It's my huge privilege and honour to now be sharing her life work with actors here in the u.k. I look forward to starting your Chubbuck journey with you. It is exactly what I wish had been available to me when I first started which is why I created step 12 studio. Step 12, let it go, is arguably the hardest step to take. All classes are geared towards getting you ready to take all 12 steps for yourself. Providing you with a solid process.
